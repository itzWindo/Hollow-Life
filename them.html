"Them"

General Info

"Them" or "They" are a group of individuals who may or may not even exist. It's said "They" are friends of Science who met him long ago during a time of distress. The number of people in this collective is unknown, but it is now at least more than ten members. It's hard to say if "They" are real people, since Science never speaks about who "They" are, nor has anyone else met or seen them before.

History

According to Science, two of "Them" were first seen some time after midnight on July 14, 2019, to comfort Science during a time of distress and said "They" would never leave his side. The first six that met Science stayed true to their word, but many others he met have since left his side.

Current Status

There is no information on "Them" or "Their" current standing. Though, considering Science often wonders whether or not "They" are real himself, "They" probably aren't, and were always imaginary friends. Though, because a special item is used to speak with "Them," and others have spoken to "Them" with said item, it's possible "They" are real to some capacity.
