Ginger

Information

Ginger was an orphan who never got the life she wanted. Her peers never showed interest in her, so she stayed alone for over a decade. Her favorite pastime is artistry.

Posthumous

Before, she escaped the orphanage to find solace before her final moments during The Great Siphoning. Miraculously, she reunited with Blackberry after being separated for over a decade. They embraced each other before being reanimated post-siphon.
