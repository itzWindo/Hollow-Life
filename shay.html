Shay

Description

Shay is a feral 300-centimeter-tall demi-human who finds pleasure in creating music. She can transform into a more innocuous form when going out in public during the day.

Occupation

After graduating from college to earn her nursing degree, she focused solely on producing music for the world, playing VRChat, and spending her time with her now-deceased partner.

Whereabouts

Shay spends the rest of her days secluded in a cave, only coming out to find food. During a blood moon, she will kill those she comes across with the large stalactites she finds in her cave. She has abandoned her home after surviving The Great Siphoning.
